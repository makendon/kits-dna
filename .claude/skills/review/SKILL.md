# Review command

Read the blog post file fresh before starting.

Review this blog post draft for:

- Sentences over 20 words — flag with line number
- Complex phrases that could be simpler
- Adverb overuse
- Passive voice
- Clarity and readability issues
- Weak transitions
- Filler words to cut

Provide specific line-by-line suggestions with explanations.

Fix mechanical errors directly in the file: typos, duplicate words, missing punctuation, and capitalisation (e.g. lowercase "i"). These have one right answer and don't affect voice.

Preserve the author's voice — don't rewrite, just flag and suggest everything else.
